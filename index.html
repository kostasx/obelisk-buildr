Welcome to obelisk buildr!

This little experiment makes use of the great obelisk.js library.

  • Everything is driven by shortcuts here, no UI, I'm lazy :)
    Press H to list all of them.

  • You can draw on 20 layers of height. Scroll your mouse up or down to adjust elevation.

  • Available tools are brush (B) and erase (E).

  • Press the spacebar to spawn a 9 colors palette. Colors are also accessible form 1 to 9.

  • Rotate the view with ← and → arrow keys

  • Your work is automatically saved locally in the browser.
    Press CTRL + S to save it to a github gist. If you log in, you will be able to fork and edit your own gists. If not, anonymous gists will be created each time you save! So please consider logging in.

  • Press any key to continue...
